College Planning

Welcome! I hope that this site will be useful in planning your educational future.
After registering, you will be able to add:

  • School Name
  • Location
  • Application Deadlines
  • Pros & Cons of each location

You'll be able to come back to review or add to your entries.
You can search either by school name or from a list of schools you have added.
